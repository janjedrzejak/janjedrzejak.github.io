6. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

6.1 Right of Access (Art. 15 GDPR)

You have the right to request information about what data I process about you.

How: Send an email to jan.jedrzejak@gmail.com
Response time: 30 days

6.2 Right to Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)

If you believe the data about you is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request correction.

6.3 Right to Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data, unless:

  • The data is necessary to fulfill a contract
  • I must retain it due to legal obligations
  • Processing is justified for other public interests

6.4 Right to Restrict Processing (Art. 18 GDPR)

You can request restriction of data processing in specific cases.

6.5 Right to Data Portability (Art. 20 GDPR)

You have the right to receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (e.g., CSV) and transfer it to another controller.

6.6 Right to Object (Art. 21 GDPR)

You have the right to object to processing based on your explicit consent.

6.7 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of the above rights, send an email to: jan.jedrzejak@gmail.com

In your message, include:

  • Which right you want to exercise
  • Your full name
  • Email address for contact
  • Detailed description if possible

I guarantee a response within 30 days.
