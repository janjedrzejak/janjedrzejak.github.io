Last Updated: November 17, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website saves on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone). They are used to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Track activity on the website
  • Ensure security
  • Personalize content

2. What Cookies Do We Use?

My website uses three categories of cookies:

These cookies are mandatory for the site to function and do not require your consent:
