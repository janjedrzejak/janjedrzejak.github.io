AI will not replace project management. It will expose weak project systems.

18 JUL 20268 MINJAN JĘDRZEJAK

AI will automate fragments of project work.

Automation amplifies the system it enters

A good system produces faster learning when AI is added.

The valuable use cases are decision-adjacent

The highest leverage is not replacing the project manager.

Practical test

If nobody knows who owns the decision without AI, the model will not create accountability.

A stronger role, not a smaller one

Project and product leaders will spend less time moving information.
